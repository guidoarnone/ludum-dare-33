Oryx's Undersized Breakout





Description

This is a small game for the Ludum Dare Jam 33. This year's theme was "You are the monster".

This game is about a little alien monster and his journey to escape his evil captors.

If you're on Chrome, UnityWebPlayer won't work unless you do "chrome://flags/#enable-npapi" first. You can disable this after playing.

Controls

  • Move with WASD
  • Spacebar lets you jump.
  • Hide from scary stuff with Q. This is only allowed in special places.
